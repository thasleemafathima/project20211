Live Action Role Playing (LARP)

LARPs are inspired by tabletop role-playing games and outdoor games for scouts.

Laog is short for live action online game

and that says it all, doesn’t it? We can larp – live action role-playing – when being behind our video camera.

Live action is for everybody

Larping online makes larping accesible to a completely new audience, makes it much easier for those who need a more flexible scheduling option.
