Shawn Roske

Shawn Roske has spent over a decade on the front lines of health care and education. He has worked in group homes for developmentally disabled adults. He has worked in schools supporting teachers and students in autism units, behavioural support programs, and developmental disability programs. His masters degree is in anthropology where he focused on pedagogy in new religious movements. He has post-graduate certification in behavioural science and autism studies. He has studied western yoga and shamanism for twenty years.

Services Provided

Tabletop role-playing games, live action role-playing games, and improv exercises are tremendously powerful tools of active learning. We offer instruction on their use and are actively working towards establishing educational curriculums with active learning foundations.
